# Equinox

- Space and time when the plane of the [[Earth]]'s equator passes through the geometric center of the [[Sun]]'s disc
  - The moment the center of the visible [[Sun]] is directly above the [[Earth]]'s equator
- Occurs twice a year
  - [[Vernal equinox]]
  - [[Autumnal equinox]]
