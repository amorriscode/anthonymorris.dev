# Helium Fusion

- Harder than [[hydrogen]] [[fusion]] ([[proton-proton chain]])
- Triple alpha process
- Only lasts for ~10% of a [[star]]s [[main sequence]] lifetime

## Process

1. ${}^4He + {}^4He + {}^4He \rarr {}^{12}C + \gamma$
2. ${}^4He + {}^{12}C \rarr {}^{16}O + \gamma$
