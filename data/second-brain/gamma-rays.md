# Gamma Rays

- Radioactive [[atom]]s and nuclear explosions
- Not many natural sources of them
- Short [[wavelength]], high [[energy]]
- Dangerous to living tissues
- Absorbed by [[Earth]]'s [[atmosphere]] before reaching the ground
