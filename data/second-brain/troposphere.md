# Troposphere

- Lowest layer of the [[Earth]]'s [[atmosphere]]
- Warm air replaced by currents of cooler air
  - [[Convection]]
  - Generates clouds and wind
- Where weather happens
- Cooler as you go up
  - Heater by the Earth's surface
- 75% of the [[atmosphere]]'s mass
