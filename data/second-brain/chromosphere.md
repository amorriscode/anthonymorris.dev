# Chromosphere

- Sphere of colour
  - Gives sun a reddish glow due to emission of [[hydrogen]] spectral lines
- Can only be seen during a total [[eclipse]]
- Roughly 2000 km thick
- 10,000 K
  - Hotter than the [[photosphere]]
