# Transistor

- Device that acts either:
  - Wire conducting [[electricity]]
  - [[Resistor]] that blocks the flow of [[electricity]]
    - Depending on voltage level of an input signal
- No moving parts
- Acts like a switch
- Made of a [[semiconductor]] material
