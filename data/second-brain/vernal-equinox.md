# Vernal (Spring) Equinox

- Around March 20
- Celestial equivalent of [[Prime Meridian]]
- Right ascension --> 0 hours
- Declination --> +23.5˚
- Sun rises at 90˚ azimuth and sets at 270˚ azimuth
