# Solar Wind

- Produced by the [[Sun]]
- Charged particles flowing outward
  - Mainly [[proton]]s and [[electron]]s
  - 400 km per second
- Gases in the corona are so hot and moving so rapidly that they cannot be held back by solar [[gravity]]
