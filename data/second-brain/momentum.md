# Momentum

- Depends on three things:
  - [[Speed]]
  - Direction of motion
  - [[Mass]]
- Can only change under the action of an outside influence

## Formula

- $p = mv$
  - $p$ is momentum
  - $m$ is [[mass]]
  - $v$ is [[velocity]]
