# Interstellar Medium

- Stuff between [[star]]s
- The [[matter]] from which [[star]]s are made
- Roughly 90% [[hydrogen]], 9% [[helium]] and 1% everything else (ionized gases)
  - Everything else includes [[atom]]s, molecules, and dust grains
