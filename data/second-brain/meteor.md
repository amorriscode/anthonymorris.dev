# Meteor

- Particles burning up in the [[Earth]]'s atmosphere
- Often referred to as shooting stars
- Differences
  - Meteoroids --> In [[space]]
  - Meteors --> In our [[atmosphere]]
  - Meteorites --> On [[Earth]]
- Formed with [[the Solar System]]
- Types
  - Stony
    - 95%
    - Look like regular rocks
  - Iron
    - Metallic [[iron]], [[nickel]], [[iridium]]
