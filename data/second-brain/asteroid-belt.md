# Asteroid Belt

- Located roughly between [[Jupiter]] and [[Mars]]
- Occupied by many solid, irregularly shaped objects
  - [[Asteroid]]s or minor [[planet]]s
  - Rocky composition
- Typical spacing between objects is several million km
