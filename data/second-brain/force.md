# Force

- An action capable of accelerating an object
- Way to change [[momentum]]
- Measured in newtons ($N$)
  - $1 N = 1 kg \times \frac{m}{s^2}$

## Formula

- $\Sigma\vec{F} = m\vec{a}$
  - $\Sigma\vec{F}$ is net force
  - $m$ is [[mass]]
  - $\vec{a}$ is [[acceleration]]
